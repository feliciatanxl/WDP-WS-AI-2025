About Leaf Plant

Cultivating Quality, Nurturing Community

For over a decade, Leaf Plant has been committed to sustainable farming and community well-being in Singapore. We specialize in producing high-quality, environmentally friendly crops through innovative practices that protect soil, conserve water, and support biodiversity.

Our journey started with a simple mission: to bring the freshest, most affordable produce directly to our community. Today, we continue to push boundaries, delivering solutions that fuel healthy living and promote a sustainable future for local agriculture.

At our core, we're a team of dedicated farmers, agronomists, and local food advocates united by a passion for ethical growing practices and creating a lasting, positive impact.
